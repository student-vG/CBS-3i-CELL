About The College

Chetan Business School + CBS 3i-Cell

The Industry Institute Interface (3i-Cell) connects academic excellence with real-world placement outcomes. We focus on building employability, leadership, and market-ready skill sets.

Industry-Ready Curriculum Live projects, case studies, and corporate mentorship.
Our placement cell is designed to guide students from career discovery to offer acceptance. With structured training and data-driven placement mapping, every student receives clear direction.
  • Skill assessments aligned with recruiter expectations.
  • Aptitude and communication labs for interview readiness.
  • Dedicated mentorship for resumes, portfolios, and GD.
  • Corporate connects through internships and live projects.

Placement Process

A Clear, Detailed Placement Journey

Each stage is tracked inside the portal so students know exactly what to prepare, submit, and complete.

01

Registration & Eligibility

Students create profiles, upload documents, and validate academic eligibility.

  • Document verification and profile approval
  • Eligibility scorecard and program mapping
  • Career interest selection
02

Skill Gap Analysis

Assessment of aptitude, communication, and domain strengths.

  • Mock tests and diagnostic quizzes
  • Resume review and feedback loop
  • Soft-skill workshops
03

Opportunity Mapping

Roles are tagged based on skills, scores, and recruiter requirements.

  • Role eligibility auto-matching
  • Company-specific prep guides
  • Placement calendar visibility
04

Application & Shortlisting

One-click applications and transparent shortlisting updates.

  • Resume submission tracking
  • Shortlist notifications
  • Interview schedule alerts
05

Selection Rounds

Assessments, group discussions, and interviews handled in sequence.

  • Aptitude, technical, and HR rounds
  • Feedback and improvement tasks
  • Offer readiness checklist
06

Offer & Onboarding

Offer letters, joining documentation, and placement closure.

  • Offer acceptance tracking
  • Joining kit documentation
  • Alumni onboarding support
