About Chetan Business School (CBS)

Chetan Business School (CBS), established in 2011 in Hubballi, is committed to high-quality management and technology education. Our flagship two-year MBA and MCA programs are AICTE approved, ISO accredited, and affiliated with Karnatak University.

Along with core MBA and MCA programs, CBS offers specialized MBA pathways to create broader advanced learning opportunities. The focus is to prepare students for strong careers and meaningful contribution to industry and society.

Placement Process

Official 8-Step Placement Flow

Built from your provided placement-process information for MBA and MCA student journeys.

1. Pre-Placement Preparation

Training workshops, resume building, communication development, mock aptitude tests, and alumni sessions.

2. Registration and Eligibility Check

Students register through the placement form with eligibility checks for attendance, grades, and discipline.

3. Pre-Placement Talks (PPTs)

Company presentations covering roles, culture, compensation, growth paths, and student Q&A sessions.

4. Application and Shortlisting

Online/offline applications with shortlisting based on academics, skills, certifications, and assessments.

5. Placement Rounds

Selection rounds may include aptitude, technical/domain interviews, GD, HR interviews, and role-fit evaluation.

6. Offer Rollout and Acceptance

Offer letter issuance, acceptance timelines, and completion of pre-joining document formalities.

7. Internships (MBA/MCA)

Many companies offer internships; strong internship performance can convert into pre-placement offers.

8. Post-Placement Support

Joining support, etiquette guidance, alumni network help, and continued placement-cell mentoring.
